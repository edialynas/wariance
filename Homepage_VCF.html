VCFtools

... is a program package designed for working with VCF files. The aim of VCFtools is to provide easily accessible methods for working with complex genetic variation data in the form of VCF files. The variant call format (VCF) is a generic format for storing DNA polymorphism data such as SNPs, insertions, deletions and structural variants, together with rich annotations. VCF is usually stored in a compressed manner and can be indexed for fast data retrieval of variants from a range of positions on the reference genome.

Guidance

The online aspect of data analysis tools is a feature you should not miss. Solutions for online data analysis enable you to access your analysis results, dashboards, reports and anything you are working on, at any time and from anywhere. Such flexibility and accessibility is a great advantage in a world where the information circulates faster than ever before. Online data analytics tools, like the one we offer, enable you to be in charge of the entire analytical process, and to therefore make better decisions at speed.
There are certain steps in our website to follow in order to use the tools we provide. Make sure to register first, and with your account information, use the log in page to sign in your MySQL database account and view your dashboard page where you can start an analysis, view jobs, if any submitted, and their status as well.

The user may choose among these procedures for their analysis:

  1. Detection of common SNPs
  2. Detection of all non common SNPs
  3. Detection of SNPs found in the one file but not in the other and vice versa.

A detailed guide, with examples to help you get started, can be found here.
